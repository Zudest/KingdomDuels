**Pyramid of Light** is the new set released in Kingdom Duels on 2026-05-14.

#### Breakdown

- Codename: **[PYL]**
- It has **158 cards** from episodes [116 to 123](/story/world-of-memories-arc/) of the Yugioh Scrolls project.
- It is focused on a reinterpretation of the "Pyramid of Light" movie alongside original content that explores the last two Millennium Items that the Pharaoh needs before his final journey. We will follow a brand new character called Fizdis (which actually comes from the old yugioh videogames!) as she has some unfinished business with the mysterious Shadi. Iconic cards like Blue-Eyes Shining Dragon, Magician Valkyria, Sorcerer of Dark Magic and, of course, the Pyramid of Light make their debut here.
- This set provides support for the following duelists: Yugi, Anzu, Jonouchi, Honda, Bakura, Haga, Ryuzaki, Kaiba, Fizdis, Sugoroku, Shadi, Marik, Ishizu, Rishid, Anubis.
