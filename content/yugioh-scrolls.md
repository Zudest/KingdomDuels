**Yugioh Scrolls** is a fanmade re-telling of the Yu-Gi-Oh! anime in a bullet point script format that uses the **[Kingdom Duels](/about)** rules and answers the question: *‚Äúwhat would have happened if the anime kept going with all the weird rules from Duelist Kingdom instead of the real rules?‚Äù.*

The story is divided into **Chapters** which are argumental arcs with a different protagonist each time (you can view them as different ‚Äúanime series‚Äù that take place in the same world and continuity, an alternative universe to the one we are familiar with). Characters, duels, plot points and entire arcs are changed in this project to tell the same story that we know and love but with a different twist.

## Notes

- This is my own take on the yugioh story so take it as that, nothing more, nothing less. It‚Äôs essentially a fanfiction that takes advantage of a card variant that we created based on the very first rules of this anime we fans saw as kids.
- Each chapter roughly represent an anime series, which is further divided into Story Arcs and Episodes. 
- The script is condensing duels usually to a single episode for convenience rather than to follow a television script format, so duels that took 4 episodes in the real anime are usually condensed into 1 episode here.
