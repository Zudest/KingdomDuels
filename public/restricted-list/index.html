Restricted List

A player must follow the Deckbuilding rules while getting ready to play. The Restricted List defines certain restrictions for deckbuilding regarding which unique cards you are allowed to include in your decks, and how many copies of them. A unique card is represented by the card code, not the card name.

⛔ Forbidden

You are not able to include cards from this list in your decks. If a combo is listed it means that you cannot include ALL the combo-pieces together in your decks, but you might include the individual pieces of the combo.

  • None

  • [TEG-006] → Ritual of the Sun of God Dragon

✅ Surplus

You can have more than 1 of each of these cards in your deck.

  • [SOE-072] → Fusion → Up to 4 copies.
  • [SOE-079] → Harpie Lady → Up to 4 copies.
  • [NPH-010] → Spirit Message → Up to 7 copies.
