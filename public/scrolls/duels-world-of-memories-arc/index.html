EP 116 - Jonouchi + Honda vs Yugi + Anzu

Player Action LP Notes
Setup Duel in progress. Tag Duel rules. Tabletop terrain (100% Land) 500 - 100 - 900 - 400
Setup Yugi has Dark Magician in ATK position, Pyro Clock of Destiny set and Dark Magic Inheritance set.
Setup Anzu has Petit Angel in face-up DEF and Numinous Healer set face down.
Setup Jonouchi has Total Defense Shogun in face-up DEF.
Setup Honda has The Allied Forces, with Magic Reflector attached to it, and Special Soldier of Science in face-up DEF.
Player 4 Anzu draws. She is excited and summons Dark Magician Girl in ATK (2000), which she says that she got in a booster pack the other day.
Player 4 Dark Magician Girl (2000) attacks Honda's Special Soldier of Science (800 DEF), but he activates his set Double Snare in return. However, Yugi activates his own Seven Tools of the Bandit, paying 500 LP to negate that trap. The attack continues, Dark Magician Girl destroys Special Soldier of Science. 500 - 100 - 400 - 400
Player 4 Anzu then activates Numinous Healer to replenish Yugi's LP. 500 - 100 - 900 - 400
Player 1 Jonouchi draws. He equips Lightning Blade to his Total Defense Shogun who had 200 ATK for The A. Forces (1550/2500 → 1750 → 2350).
Player 1 Total Defense Shogun in DEF (2500) attacks Dark Magician Girl (2000) using its ATK (2350) due to its effect. Yugi activates his set Pyro Clock of Destiny to move forward the entire round and continue where they were, and chain to it Dark Magic Inheritance.
Player 2 Turn skipped due to Pyro Clock of Destiny effect.
Player 3 Turn skipped due to Pyro Clock of Destiny effect.
Player 4 Turn skipped due to Pyro Clock of Destiny effect.
Player 1 Portion of his turn is skipped due to Pyro Clock of Destiny effect. The attack continues, his Total Defense Shogun (2350) is attacking Dark Magician Girl (2000). Yugi destroys Dark Magic Inheritance and its effect activates, increasing her ATK by 1000. Total Defense Shogun is destroyed. 650 LP self-damage. 0 - 100 - 900 - 400
Player 1 Yugi and Anzu win due to Tag Duel rules.

EP 117 - Bakura vs Haga + Ryuzaki

Player Action LP Notes
Setup Boss Battle rules. Dark Alley terrain (80% City, 20% Night) 2000 - 2000 Haga and Ryuzaki has just stolen Yugi's backpack that contains the Millennium Items and the Egyptian Gods.
Player 2 Haga draws. He summons Roachball in ATK (1200) and sets two cards.
Player 1 Bakura draws. He summons Ghost Earl in ATK in the Night terrain (2000 → 2600) but Haga activates his set Parasite Worm and attaches to it, also activating the Rise of the Parasite Rule so it loses field power bonus (2600 → 2000).
Player 3 Ryuzaki draws. He summons Megasaurus in ATK (1800) and passes due to Haga's instructions.
Player 1 Bakura draws. He sets a card and passes.
Player 2 Haga draws. He summons Hunter Spider in ATK (1600) that gains ATK due to the opponent not being able to move (1600 → 2100). He then activates his Lair Wire, discarding Winged Cleaver from his hand, forcing the opponent to Special Summon a monster from his hand if they have a monster that cannot move.
Player 2 Bakura is forced to summon The One Lurking in the Painting in ATK (1200).
Player 2 Hunter Spider (1600 → 2100) attacks and destroys Ghost Earl (2000). 100 LP damage. 1900 - 2000
Player 2 Due to Lair Wire effect, Haga can attack once again. Hunter Spider (1600 → 2100) attacks and destroys The One Lurking in the Painting (1200). 900 LP damage. Haga sets a card and end his turn. 1000 - 2000
Player 1 Bakura draws. He hides a monster in the Night terrain, and he flips his You Reap What You Sow to inflict 1500 LP of damage to them. He then sets a card. 1000 - 500
Player 3 Ryuzaki draws. He is getting tired of not doing anything. Haga tells him to be cautious and don't attack, but he wants to do it. Megasaurus (1800) attacks the hidden monster but it is Metamor Pot. They all discard their hands and draw 5 new cards. Haga and Ryuzaki fight over this.
Player 3 Ryuzaki summons Trakodon (1300) and sets a card.
Player 1 Bakura draws. He summons face-up Dark King of the Abyss in the Night terrain (1200 → 1560) and then activates "A Deal with Dark Ruler", offering Ryuzaki a choice. Either he attacks his Dark King of the Abyss or he attacks Haga's Roachball (inflicting no damage but earning them LP).
Player 1 Ryuzaki agrees, and uses his Megasaurus (1800) to destroy Haga's Roachball, which activates his set Mimesis, returning Roachball to his hand and forcing him to summon 4-Starred Ladybug of Doom, his only monster in hand after his hand got replenished, who gets destroyed. They gain 500 LP. 1000 - 1000
Player 1 Bakura then activates A Deal with Dark Ruler once again, offering the choice to Haga this time. He takes it, attacking Trakodon (1300) with his Hunter Spider (1600), even when Ryuzaki was telling him not to do it. As this was treated as an opponent attack, it triggers Ryuzaki's set trap Metal Morph, turning Trakodon into Cyber Saurus (1800). Hunter Spider is destroyed. No damage inflicted, and they gain 500 LP. 1000 - 1500
Player 1 Bakura then laughs and activates his Bad Reaction to Simochi as a spell, retroactively turning that LP gain into damage by halving their LP each time. As they gained LP two times this turn, their LP first goes back to 500 after negating their gain, then divided to 250 and then divided to 125. 1000 - 125
Player 1 Bakura activates Sword of Dark Destruction equipping it to Dark King of the Abyss (1560 → 2160). It attacks and destroys Cyber Saurus (1800). 360 LP inflicted. 1000 - 0
Player 1 Bakura wins. Bakura takes away the bag from them just in time when Yugi arrives. Bakura then returns the objects to him.

EP 117 - Bakura vs Kaiba

Player Action LP Notes
Setup Duel in Apex Arena (Sky 40%, Arena 60%) 2000 - 2000
Player 1 Bakura draws. He activates Fusion to Fusion Summon The Duke of Demise (2000) by combining Ghost Earl and Grudge Aggregate from his hand.
Player 2 Kaiba draws. He summons Blood Vorse in ATK (1900). He activates Shrink to halve the attack of the duke (2000 → 1000), and attacks it. Due to its effect, it cannot be destroyed in that battle. 900 LP damage. 1100 - 2000
Player 1 Bakura draws. He pays 500 LP to keep The Duke on the field. 600 - 2000
Player 1 He then uses his Diabound Ritual to Ritual Summon Diabound in ATK in the Sky Terrain (1800 → 2340). He then activates Spirit Illusion targeting his Diabound and creating a clone of it.
Player 1 He activates the effect of the cloned Diabound, equipping it to Blood Vorse and reducing its ATK by its level times 100 (1900 → 1500). Diabound (2340) attacks and destroys it. 840 LP damage. He then sets a card. 600 - 1160
Player 2 Kaiba draws. He summons Lord of Dragons in face-up DEF (1100) and uses his Flute of Summoning Dragons to Special Summon one Blue-Eyes White Dragon from his hand in the sky terrain (3000 → 3900).
Player 2 Blue-Eyes White Dragon (3900) attacks Diabound (2340) but Bakura uses his set Spirit Shield, removing one Undead monster from his graveyard to negate that attack. Kaiba sets a card.
Player 1 Bakura draws. He uses Pot of Greed to draw two cards. He then uses Grief Tablet targeting Lord of Dragons.
Player 1 He then uses the effect of Spirit Illusion once again to create a token and weaken Blue-Eyes by 800 and lose its field power bonus (3900 → 3000 → 2200).
Player 1 Diabound (2340) attacks Blue Eyes (2200) but Kaiba uses his Attack Guidance Armor to redirect the attack to his Lord of Dragons, destroying it (and also the Grief Tablet). Kaiba loses 200 LP due to Grief Tablet's destruction. 600 - 960
Player 1 Bakura sets two cards.
Player 2 Kaiba draws. He activates Life Shaving Treasure Cards to draw until he has five cards in hand. He then uses Soul Release to erode from Bakura's graveyard all Undead monsters there. With them gone, the Spirit Shield is also destroyed.
Player 2 He then summons his second Blue-Eyes White Dragon (3000 → 3900). It attacks and destroys Diabound (2340). Bakura activates his set Rebirth Tablet to revive it, and then activates his Mirror Tablet so Diabound gains 900 attack (2340 → 3140), Blue-Eyes loses its Field Power Bonus (3900 → 3000). Kaiba changes his other Blue-Eyes to DEF and sets a card.
Player 1 Bakura draws. Diabound (3140) attacks and destroys Blue-Eyes White Dragon (3000). 140 LP damage. 600 - 820
Player 1 Kaiba then activates his set Resurrection of the Dead to revive his Blue-Eyes (3000 → 3900).
Player 1 At this point, Bakura leaves the duel. It ends in a no-result. DNF

EP 118 - Shadi vs Fizdis + Sugoroku

Player Action LP Notes
Setup Duel in Pyramid Maze (Desert 40%, Ruins 60%) 2000 - 2000 A ghost of Shadi materializes in front of them, a trap setup by him to put obstacles in their way, and sets up a duel
Player 1 Shadi draws and plays Judgment of the Land, which activates his Rule card "Mind Puzzle - Justice of the Land", and therefore a subduel happens. A subduel begins here. Player 5 = Shadi, Player 6 = the Group (Fizdis + Sugoroku, playing together)
Player 6 Group's Standby Phase. All Terrains are "Land" Terrains. Shadi has "Peten the Dark Clown" in DEF equipped with "Complete Destruction Genocide Virus". The Group controls "Empress of Irreversible Justice" in ATK and "Judgment Hawk" in ATK. They have "Trial of Hell", "Meteorain", and "Nightmare Iron's Binding" in hand. They have "Curse of Darkness", "Sword of Dark Destruction" in graveyard. (500 - 500) Subduel starts.
Player 6 The Group plays "Trial of Hell", which allows them to equip a card to Peten the Dark Clown from their graveyard. They equip Sword of Dark Destruction, which increases its ATK by 600 (500 → 1100). This results in its ATK being higher than 500 so the Complete Destruction Genocide Virus card is destroyed on its own effect. Subduel.
Player 6 The Group activate Meteorain. Empress of Irreversible Justice (2100) attacks Peten in DEF (1200 DEF - 200 (for the sword) → 1000 DEF) = 1100 damage halved by meteorain and inflicted as half-piercing damage. 550 LP damage to Shadi. The Subduel ends. (0 - 500) Subduel.
Player 1 Due to the effect of "Judgment of the Land", Shadi loses 2000 LP. 0 - 2000
Player 1 Fizdis and Sugoroku win.

EP 119 - Ahmed (Rishid) vs Sugoroku

Player Action LP Notes
Setup Duel in Pyramid Maze (Desert 40%, Ruins 60%) 2000 - 2000 As an illusion, Sugoroku sees the spirits of Ahmed and Mushara (his guides who betrayed him in Egypt a long time ago) instead of Rishid and Anzu, who got separated from the others. They, in turn, see him as another of Shadi's traps.
Player 1 Rishid draws. He sets three cards and plays Temple of the Royal Family, special summoning Sarcophagus Token in DEF (0). Sugoroku is surprised by this card, but thinks is fitting that the brothers play Egyptian themed cards.
Player 2 Sugoroku draws. he plays Walls of the Imperial Tomb. Then he plays King's Sarcophagus. The combination of those cards allows him to Ritual Summon one of each of the four sons of Horus per turn. He Ritual Summons Hapi, Guidance of Horus (2400) by eroding four cards from the bottom of his deck. Rishid and Anzu see fitting that what they see as Shadi is playing Egyptian themed cards.
Player 2 Hesitant at first, Sugoroku orders Hapi (2400) to attack the Sarcophagus Token (0 DEF), but Rishid activates his set Defense of the Temple to negate the attack and add 1 Apophis card to his hand.
Player 2 At the end of the Battle Phase, Rishid activates his set Embodiment of Apophis, Special Summon it to the frontrow. Then he sets a card in that column due to Embodiment of Apophis effect. Sugoroku sets a card.
Player 1 Rishid draws. He activates the card he set in the previous turn, summoning Apophis the Swamp Deity. Then he uses Fusion, to Fusion Summon Divine Serpent Apophis (2800).
Player 1 Apophis (2800) attacks and destroys Hapi (2400). 400 LP damage. 2000 - 1600
Player 2 Sugoroku draws. He Ritual Summons Imsety, Glory of Horus (3000) by eroding four cards from the bottom of his deck.
Player 2 Due to Imsety effect, Sugoroku special summons Curse of Dragon from his hand, in face-up DEF. Anzu is surprised to see Curse of Dragon being played by "Shadi"
Player 2 Imsety is about to attack, but suddenly Yugi arrives and sees them fighting, with their eyes clouded by magic (he does not see them as illusions). With his millennium puzzle he breaks the illusion present in the room. At this point, the duel ends in a no-result, as they finally see each other
Player 2 The duel is interrupted DNF

Player 1 Shadi draws. He summons Destroyer Golem in the Ruins terrain in ATK (1500 → 1950). It attacks Reactor Slime but Marik uses his set Torture Wheel to prevent that battle and trap the golem.
Player 1 Shadi sets two cards and ends his turn.
Player 2 Marik draws. During the Standby Phase the Torture Wheel inflicts 250 LP of damage to Shadi, and Marik Special Summons 1 Magic Slime from his graveyard in DEF (500 DEF) due to Reactor Slime. 1750 - 2000
Player 2 He then summons Slime King in ATK (1000) and ends his turn.
Player 1 Shadi draws. He uses The Forceful Sentry to return his own Destroyer Golem to the deck, which in turn sends Torture Wheel to Marik's deck. Both players draw one card.
Player 1 Shadi summons Absoluter (1300) and equips it with Duty of the Millennium Absoluter.
Player 1 Absoluter attacks and destroys the Slime King. 300 LP damage. 1750 - 1700
Player 2 Marik draws. He special summons 1 Slime Token due to Reactor Slime. He then activates Slime Guardian Ritual to Ritual Summon Guardian Slime in ATK (0) by tributing his Slime Token.
Player 2 Guardian Slime (0 → 1400) attacks and destroys Absoluter (1300). 100 LP damage. Marik sets a card, and Guardian Slime (1400 → 0) switches to DEF in the End Phase. 1650 - 1700
Player 1 Shadi draws. He uses Treasure Cards from the Heaven so both players draw until they have six cards in hand.
Player 1 Then he summons Millennium Golem in the Ruins terrain (2000 → 2600) and equips it with Duty of the Golem of the Millennium Treasures.
Player 1 The Golem (2600) attacks the Reactor, but the attack is redirected to Guardian Slime (0 → 2000) due to Protector rule. Guardian Slime is destroyed.
Player 1 After its destruction, Marik activates his set Resurrection of the Slime to bring back in his next Main Phase his slime monster.
Player 2 Marik draws. He activates Resurrection of the Slime to bring back Guardian Slime (0 ATK). He special summons 1 Slime Token due to Reactor Slime.
Player 2 He then activates the Reactor effect to fuse the Magical Slime with the Plasma Eel in his hand to create Plasma Slime Eel (500 ATK) which immediately attaches itself to the Millennium Golem, weakening it.
Player 2 He then uses Slimeball, inflicting 150 LP and Special Summoning a Slime Token in that Terrain in DEF, under Shadi's control. 1500 - 1700
Player 1 Shadi draws. He summons Maiden of the Moonlight in DEF (1300 DEF) and equips her with Duty of the Maiden of the Millennium Moon, activating that effect to return the Golem to his deck, which in turn destroys the equipped Plasma Slime Eel. This effect allows him to Special Summon Millennium Scorpion to the field.
Player 1 Millennium Scorpion gains 500 due to Shadi's LP being lower than Marik (2000 → 2500), it attacks and destroys the Guardian Slime (0 → 2000). 500 LP damage 1500 - 1200
Player 2 Marik draws. He special summons 1 Slime Token due to Reactor Slime. Then he revives his Slime King in ATK (1000) due to Resurrection of the Slime. He sets a card.
Player 1 Shadi draws. He uses Magic Removal to destroy Resurrection of the Slime. His Millennium Scorpion (2000) then attacks and destroys Slime King (1000). 1000 LP damage. 1500 - 200
Player 1 As Slime King was destroyed, Marik activates Reforming Slime, tributing a Slime Token to add 1 "Slime" card from his deck to his hand. He adds Slimetamorphosis.
Player 2 Marik draws. He special summons 1 Slime Token due to Reactor Slime.
Player 2 Then he uses Lava Devil Ritual to Ritual Summon Lava Golem in Shadi's field by tributing his Maiden of the Moonlight and Millennium Scorpion.
Player 1 Shadi draws. Lava Golem (3000) attacks and destroys Reactor Slime (500 DEF).
Player 1 In the End Phase, Shadi loses 400 LP due to Lava Golem. 1100 - 200
Player 2 Marik draws. He uses Rise and Slime to bring back Shadi's Maiden of the Moonlight (1500) and equip her with it (1500 → 2100).
Player 2 Then he uses Slimetamorphosis targeting Lava Golem and a Magic Slime in his graveyard, Fusion Summoning Slime Golem in its place. The Maiden (2100) attacks Slime Golem (3000), but due to Rise and Slime she cannot be destroyed in battle with a Slime monster, and due to Slime Golem effect, damage is inflicted to Shadi. 900 LP damage to Shadi. 200 - 200
Player 2 At this point, after proving himself, Marik uses another copy of Slimeball, but this time in its own Terrain, inflicting 200 LP to himself and to Shadi, effectively ending the duel in a draw. 0 - 0
Player 2 The duel ends in a draw.

EP 120 - Fizdis vs Ishizu

Player Action LP Notes
Setup Duel in Ancient Egyptian Temple (Desert 40%, Ruins 40%, Temple 20%) 2000 - 2000
Player 2 Ishizu draws. She is unsure about fighting Fizdis so she plays defensively. She summons Gravekeeper's Descendant in face-up DEF (1200 DEF) and passes her turn.
Player 1 Fizdis draws. She summons Guardian Sphinx (1700). It attacks and destroys Gravekeeper's Descendant.
Player 2 Ishizu draws. She summons Kelbek in ATK (1500) and states that Fizdis should not attack it.
Player 1 Fizdis draws. She asks if Ishizu is mocking her by playing like this. Her Guardian Sphinx attacks and destroys Kelbek. 200 LP damage. 2000 - 1800
Player 1 Due to Kelbek effect, Guardian Sphinx returns to Fizdis hand. Then, she normal summons Hieracosphinx, and due to its effect, it's ATK becomes 2400. She sets a card.
Player 2 Ishizu draws. She hides a monster in DEF (it has FLIP) and sets a card.
Player 1 Fizdis draws. She summons Criosphinx in face-up DEF (2400 DEF). Then she activates Book of Lunar Eclipse to swap the hidden monster to face-up ATK. It is flipped as A Cat of Ill Omen (500 ATK). Due to its effect, Ishizu adds a Trap to the top of her deck and due to the Cat being flipped by its opponent, each player would take half of the damage from that battle if Fizdis decides to attack the cat this turn.
Player 1 She does. Hieracosphinx (2400) attacks and destroys A Cat of Ill Omen (500). 1900 damage halved so 950 LP damage to each duelist. 1050 - 850
Player 2 Ishizu draws. She decides to focus now, and activates Necrovalley, sets a card, and summons Gravekeeper Spiritualist in ATK (1500). Spiritualist effect activates, allowing her to Special Summon Gravekeeper's Priestess in ATK (1500) from the top of her deck.
Player 1 Fizdis draws. Her Hieracosphinx (2400) attacks Gravekeeper Priestess, but Ishizu activates her set Shrine of Necrovalley, weakening her monster by 1000. Priestess (1500) destroys Hieracosphinx (2400 → 1400). 100 LP selfdamage. 950 - 850
Player 2 Ishizu draws. She uses Pot of Greed to draw two cards. She summons Gravekeeper Heretic (1800), which sends Necrovalley to the top of her deck, to Fizdis surprise.
Player 2 Ishizu, now that Necrovalley is not on the field, plays Resurrection of the Dead to bring back Gravekeeper Descendant (1500). Then, she activates Priestess effect to look at the top 3 of her deck, reveal Necrovalley and add it to her hand once again. She activates Necrovalley once again, but she doesn't have enough attack just yet to destroy Criosphinx (2400 DEF) so she passes.
Player 1 Fizdis draws. She is on the backfoot, and sets a card while thinks on a strategy.
Player 2 Ishizu draws and reveals that the card she drew was Owl of Luck, and given that she has Necrovalley on the field, she uses the Owl's effect to search for a "Necrovalley" card. She then activates Necrovalley Throne, the card she just searched. With this, she Alter Summons Gravekeeper's Oracle (2000) by Alter Summoning Gravekeeper's Descendant.
Player 2 Gravekeeper's Oracle gains 500 ATK due to its effect. Fizdis uses her set Book of Solar Eclipse in response, to change face-up monsters to hidden DEF and avoid the attack for now. In the end phase they return to normal.
Player 1 Fizdis draws. She sets a card and passes.
Player 2 Ishizu draws. She activates Necrovalley Throne to Alter Summon Gravekeeper's Shaman (1500) by Alter Summoning Gravekeeper's Priestess.
Player 2 Gravekeeper's Oracle gains (2000 → 2500) attacks and destroys Criosphinx (2400 DEF), which allows Fizdis to negate the effects of a persistent card. She negates Necrovalley Throne, because she knows that if she negates Necrovalley, Ishizu can send it to the top of the deck with her Heretic and play it back.
Player 1 Fizdis draws. She summons her original monster, Guardian Sphinx in face-up DEF (2400 DEF) and ends her turn.
Player 2 Ishizu draws. She activates Royal Tomb of Necrovalley: she pays 200 LP to Special Summon Gravekeeper's Recruiter from her deck. 950 - 650
Player 2 Gravekeeper's Oracle (2500) attacks Guardian Sphinx (2400 DEF). Fizdis activates its effect, picking a random card from her hand and making Ishizu guess it. She guesses a Trap, which is wrong, the card is revealed to be Resurrection of the Dead, which is a spell. The attack fails.
Player 2 As Fizdis didn't received damage this turn even after that battle, the Royal Tomb of Necrovalley inflicts 200 LP damage to her. 750 - 650
Player 1 Fizdis draws. She activates Hurricane, returning all backrow to the hand (including Necrovalley).
Player 1 Then, she uses Resurrection of the Dead to resurrect Hieracosphinx, who in turn activates its effect. By tributing Hieracosphinx, Fizdis alter summons Pharaonic Guardian Sphinx in ATK (2300) by using Guardian Sphinx.
Player 1 Ishizu says that no matter what, she has to attack Gravekeeper's Oracle. But Fizdis uses Book of Moon to hide it into DEF. This effectively removes the protection that it had.
Player 1 Guardian Sphinx (2300) attacks and destroys Gravekeeper's Shaman (1500). 800 LP damage. 750 - 0
Player 1 Fizdis wins.

EP 121 - Shadi vs Fizdis

Player Action LP Notes
Setup Duel in Ancient Egyptian Temple (Desert 40%, Ruins 40%, Temple 20%) 2000 - 2000
Player 1 Shadi draws. He plays Judgment of the Dead and uses its effect to activate his Rule card "Mind Puzzle - Marionette Design", and therefore a subduel happens. The same judgment he used on her uncle. A subduel begins here. Player 5 = Shadi, Player 6 = Fizdis.
Player 6 Fizdis' Standby Phase. 100% Land Terrain. No cards in graveyard or deck. Fizdis has Marionette Token in ATK, and in her hand she has "Brain Control", "Mimic Illusionist", "Stampeding Sword Beast". Shadi has Mister Bomber in DEF, no cards in hand and "Legendary Sword", "Red Medicine" and "Magic Removal" in his graveyard. (200 - 200) Subduel.
Player 6 Fizdis activate her Mimic Illusionist, targeting Red Medicine in Shadi's graveyard and choosing the effect of healing 500 LP herself. (200 - 700) Subduel.
Player 6 She then let's the duel play out without playing more cards. She enters the Battle Phase and her Marionette Token (700) has to attack Mister Bomber (900). 200 LP self-damage. (200 - 500) Subduel.
Player 6 In the End Phase, Mister Bomber's effect trigger, destroying itself and inflicting 200 LP of damage to Shadi. Shadi loses, the subduel ends. (0 - 500) Subduel ends.
Player 1 Shadi summons Absoluter in face-up DEF, sets a card and ends his turn.
